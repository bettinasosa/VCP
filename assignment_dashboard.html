Brief text explanation about what stage 0 is.

Design Engineering Futures

12 ECTS

Group project of number of 3 students in a team. Teams are generated by EOI method .

Brief

The Group Project provides the opportunity to bring together the wide range of skills and knowledge developed from the course so far and to make a major statement, not just as individual teams, but also as the whole Dyson School of Design Engineering. The team’s exploration and design in response to the overall theme of Design Engineering - technology and product service system futures will result in highly resolved design embodiments, supported with research, which collectively will form an authoritative series of future visions. Individuals and teams will end the project with powerful communications materials for portfolios and self-marketing. The value will be further enhanced through promotion of the complete body of work in the March 2018 Open House event.

Deliverables

Interim review

T1 W7
30%

A max 1.5- 2 minute video supported with initial prototyping (eg from the W6 hackathon) and Q&A session with an assignment panel. The projectmanagement and individual contributioncriteria will be assessed based on review ofthe group’s online records.

Group futures report

T1 W7
30%

A team generated – designed - visual report (max 20 A4 pages, 3000 words).

Futures embodiment, exhibition & presentation

T1 W7
40%

Exhibition of work including 3D digital and/or physical embodiment, 2D communication materials, Pecha Kucha style presentation and 5-minute formal Q&A.

Interim Review

3.6 ECTS

Group submission in a form of 1.5-2 mins video and 5 mins Presentation.
